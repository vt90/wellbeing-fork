about About

Wellbeing is an App which aims at not only connecting patients to doctors but also at helping medical professionals manage their clients / patients / work, wherein they know they’ll find personalized healthcare. It is directed towards interlinking them with everything they need, to take good care of their “wellbeing” which involves - finding the right doctor, booking diagnostic tests, obtaining required feedbacks, storing health records and all kinds of activities relevant to healthcare in general.

The WellBeing app

  • Helps you get rid of the “Waiting period” by allowing you to book an appointment well in advance and notifying you about your turn, so that you can save your valuable time and put it to good use.

  • Allows patients to Search for Doctors/Labs in their localities so that on the basis of their Location, they are provided with a list of the available labs/physicians.

  • Provides a One-Touch Access to patients to view and share their medical records with the consulting physicians.

  • Enables doctors to Add Multiple Clinics in which they would be working, along with the option to add Multiple Assistants.

  • Furnishes a systematic management of the profiles of various labs or clinics enabling patients to acquire detailed information about their services and to select the doctors/labs that best suit their requirements.

  • Aids patients to easily Search for nearby Pharmacies. This application is immensely beneficial in approaching medical institutions whenever an emergency arises.

  • Reduces and streamlines the administrative work of doctors through its systematic Appointment Schedules and Reminders panel.

  • Permits doctors to record a detailed summary of the patients’ visits and their medication under the Add Medicines Tab.
